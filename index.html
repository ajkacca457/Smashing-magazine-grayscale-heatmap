Person of the week

Zara Cooper is a software developer and technical writer running her own web design and consulting business. She loves sharing what she learns as a developer with others and has written about Angular, Hugo, Firebase, as well as about contributing to Open Source and writing technical specs. To help developers ease the process of generating design docs and tech specs, Zara created Doc, a CLI tool that uploads specs to Google Docs. You can find her on Twitter as @Zara__Cooper. Thank you for sharing and caring, dear Zara!
